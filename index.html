Our Process

5-Stage Purification Process

Every bottle of Puriva Water passes through a rigorous 5-stage purification system to ensure purity, safety, and freshness.

Step 01

Source Filtration

Removal of large sediments and impurities from raw water source.

Step 02

Reverse Osmosis

Advanced RO membrane removes dissolved salts, heavy metals & bacteria.

Step 03

UV Purification

UV rays destroy all residual micro-organisms for germ-free water.

Step 04

Ozonation

Ozone treatment ensures extended freshness and no chemical taste.

Step 05

Quality Testing

Rigorous lab testing before packaging to meet BIS standards.
